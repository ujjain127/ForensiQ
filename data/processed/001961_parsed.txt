4. LoopVerein
Why is it necessary to include all those loop corrections?
Experimental situation:
ILC →provide high accuracy measurements !
Theory situation:
measured observables have to be compared with theoretical predictions
(of your favorite model)
High precision of data can only fully be exploited if it is matched with
theoretical calculations at the same level of accuracy
We could not make full use of the great ILC precision
without corresponding loop calculations
We have to work NOW to achieve necessary accuracy in time
Theory/Loop calculations should be viewed as an essential part of
the ILC Physics program
